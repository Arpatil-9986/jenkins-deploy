Welcome to our Pharmacy

HMI Pharmacy is an NHS registered pharmacy that offers a wide range of services tailored to meet the health needs of the community.

Here at HMI Pharmacy we firmly believe that to serve the community requires a constant commitment and professional attitude towards its healthcare needs. We aim to provide our patients and customers with comprehensive information and guidance about their health and well-being.

We remain patient-focused and offer easy access to a wide range of pharmaceutical and medical supplies. All our staff continuously work hard to maintain and promote a high quality customer and healthcare service that is accessible to all individuals.

We have been nominated for the award of Independent Pharmacy of the Year!

We are here for you

HMI Pharmacy is supervised by fully qualified pharmacists who are available to discuss your medical issues and problems in a confidential environment through a face to face visit on site, email or telephone.

All our pharmacists are professionally registered with the General Pharmaceutical Council (GPhC), which is the Regulatory Body for Pharmacy in Great Britain. The premises is fully approved and regulated by UK Government Bodies. We fully adhere to the high standards as set by the GPhC and are also a member of the National Pharmacy Association (NPA). Our pharmacists follow Standard Operating Procedures and use their professional judgement to check the suitability of a medicine for a particular patient and ensure its safe delivery.

HMI Pharmacy works hard to provide its customers with the best possible care, ensuring that we are always there for you!
